About us

Since 1917, Lions have served the world's population through hard work and commitment to make a difference in the lives of people everywhere. With 1,360,121 members serving in more than 46,385 clubs in over 209 countries and geographical areas as of June 30th 2014. Lions Clubs International is the world's largest service club organization. Lions are recognised world-wide for their service to needy people.
